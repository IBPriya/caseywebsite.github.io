The Maze Runner series

This book is about a group of teenagers who are locked up in a glade surrounded by a deadly maze.
Did the yget out of the maze alive? Did Thomas the kids with the heart, lead them out? It is a great read.
I enjoyed all three books and the movie. Can't wait to ready the 4th and 5th part.

Words on Fire

This book had me sad, but still kind of liked it. It's about this girl that lives in a country where the people in charge are trying to erase her culture and their books.
The people in charge go to her house and her parents give her a package to deliver and make her leave without them.
It made me so sad when she had to leave her parents, but I liked her courage in doing what she was told.

Harry Potter

I remember reading my first Harry Potter book. It was one of my first serious books.The world and magic in the book is so cool and interesting. I wish I could ride a broom like them, but I think it might be a little scary.

Diary of Wimpy Kid

I love love love this funny book. I have ready the entire series. It is about a middle child in a family of three kids and how he copes with everyday challenges.
The family is always struggling to make ends meet and he always ends up in many unlucky streaks.
